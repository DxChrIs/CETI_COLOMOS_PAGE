HISTORY

The CETI is a regional institution that offers educational services at the upper secondary and higher levels in 3 campuses: the Colomos campus located in the metropolitan area of Guadalajara and the Tonalá and Río Santiago campuses, both located in the municipality of Tonalá, Jalisco. In recent decades, it has stood out in its environment as an educational institution dedicated to the training of professional technologists and engineers who have achieved broad recognition in social and productive sectors.
